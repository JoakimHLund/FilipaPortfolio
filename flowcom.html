Project Type: Project Exam at Noroff.

Timeframe: Spring 2024 - 5 weeks.

Main Roles: User research, facilitating workshops, wireframing and testing.

Background

The public speaker industry in Norway is highly competitive and saturated, and booking rates are still low due to the economic effects of the Covid pandemic.

FlowCom delivers lectures and workshops to companies, enhancing their employees' skills and knowledge. This company is owned by Nina Hanssen, a journalist and author with over 12 years of public speaking experience.

The Challenge

For this project I had the opportunity to work with a front-end developer and a marketing student. Together we developed a website for Nina Hanssen that showcases her work and simplifies the process of booking her for lectures and workshops.

I set out to identify the needs and goals of both public speakers and their clients. As such I conducted an exploratory interview with Nina Hanssen, literature review and collected client reviews. Lastly, I analyzed all data through affinity mapping and atomic research.
