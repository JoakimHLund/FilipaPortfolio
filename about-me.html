What should you know about me?

I'm Filipa Costa, a UX designer based in Oslo. With a master's degree in Psychology and experience working with troubled teens, I bring a unique perspective to design, focusing on empathy and user-centered solutions.

Originally from Portugal, I moved to Norway to pursue my passion for research and apply it on the UX field.

I love painting in my little art corner and playing cozy games like Animal Crossing and Stardew Valley. I’m also an avid reader with a passion for fantasy and sci-fi novels, and I never leave home without my trusty kindle.
